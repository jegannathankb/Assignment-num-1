Simple Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta - 200g
  • Tomatoes - 2
  • Olive Oil - 1 Spoon
  • Cheese - 1/2 Cup
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta for 10 minutes.
  2. Cook tomatoes with olive oil.
  3. Mix the cooked pasta with the sauce.
  4. Add cheese on the top.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
